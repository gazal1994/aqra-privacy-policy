Privacy Policy – Aqra App

Last updated: November 16, 2025

Aqra (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the Aqra mobile application (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

By using Aqra, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Login information (Google / Email authentication)

1.2 User Activity & Content

  • Messages between students and teachers
  • Bookings and lesson scheduling
  • User-generated content such as reviews, availability times, and profile details

1.3 Device Information

  • Device model and operating system
  • App usage information
  • Crash logs and performance analytics

1.4 Location Information (Optional)

We may receive coarse location data (such as city or country) in order to help match students with teachers. We do not collect precise GPS location.

2. How We Use the Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Facilitate communication between students and teachers
  • Manage bookings and scheduling
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Enhance safety and prevent fraud
  • Send notifications about lessons, bookings, and updates

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share data only with:

3.1 Service Providers

Such as:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging
  • Firebase Storage
  • Analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics for Firebase)

These services help us operate the App and provide its features.

3.2 Teachers and Students

Teachers can see limited user information (such as name, messages, and bookings) necessary to conduct lessons. Students can see teacher profiles and communication messages.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests, or to protect the rights and safety of our users and the public.

4. Data Storage & Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Your data may be stored and processed using:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore / Realtime Database
  • Firebase Storage
  • Secure encrypted connections (HTTPS)

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such data, we will delete it promptly.

6. Your Rights

You may have the right to:

  • Request access to the information we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Update or correct your personal information
  • Turn off or manage notifications

To submit a request, please contact us at: aqra.system@gmail.com

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
