Delete Your Aqra Account

If you wish to delete your Aqra account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

You can request account deletion by sending an email to our support team:

Email: aqra.system@gmail.com

Your deletion request must include:

  • Your full name
  • The email address associated with your Aqra account

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Once your request is processed, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • User profile information (name, email, photo)
  • Messages between teachers and students
  • Lesson bookings and scheduling data
  • App usage data linked to your account

Retention Period

Your account and all related data will be deleted within 30 days of your request.

If you have any additional questions, please contact us at the email above.
